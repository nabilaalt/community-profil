Welcome to Civil.Id Community

We are a community committed to empowering individuals with knowledge and resources on public policy and civic engagement.
Our mission is to inspire active participation in the policymaking process, fostering a better understanding of how governance shapes the future of our society.

Our Goals

Civic Empowerment

Civil.Id aims to empower individuals, particularly youth, to actively participate in public policy and decision-making processes.

Inclusivity

The community promotes an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives and backgrounds are welcomed in discussions on public policy.

Education and Awareness

Civil.Id focuses on providing educational resources and raising awareness about public policy to foster an informed and engaged society.
